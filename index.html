This is Animals

Animals are living organisms that belong to the biological kingdom Animalia. They are multicellular, meaning their bodies are made up of many cells, and they are usually capable of movement, although some animals, like sponges, may stay in one place. Animals are heterotrophic, meaning they must eat other organisms (plants, animals, etc.) for energy, unlike plants which produce their own food through photosynthesis.
